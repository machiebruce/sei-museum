Eleonora's Falcon is a fascinating raptor, specialized in hunting small migratory birds. Its name is dedicated to Eleonora d'Arborea, a Sardinian judge who in the 14th century issued laws for the protection of falcons.

#### **Distribution and Habitat**

**Global Distribution**
Breeds almost exclusively on Mediterranean islands (Greece, Italy, Spain) and on the Atlantic coasts of Morocco and the Canary Islands. Winters in Madagascar.

**Preferred Habitat**
Steep sea cliffs, inaccessible cliffs on islands.

**Presence in Sicily**
Localized breeding on minor islands (Egadi, Eolie, Pelagie, Ustica) and on coastal cliffs.

**Status in the Salt Pans**
Frequent visitor during late summer and autumn. From the Egadi islands it moves toward the Trapani coast to hunt tired birds or those in transit over the salt pans.
